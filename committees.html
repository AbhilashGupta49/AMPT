AMPT Governance and Members of the Steering Committee
AMPT Organisation's general affairs are managed by the Chairman of the Steering Committee and ratified by the members of the Committee present at its annual meeting held during the AMPT conference. The statement of account of income and expenditures during the year is also presented by the Chairman at this meeting for ratifying and approval by the Steering Committee. Each AMPT conference is attended typically by 5 to 10 members of the Steering Committee who take the decision and the chairman circulates the report to all members of the committee after the conference.

The decision on the venues of the next conferences is decided by the majority vote by the members of the Steering Committee. All Proposals (Bids) for hosting any AMPT conference are circulated to the members of the Steering Committee for voting and the venue with most support gets to be selected. The Chairman then notifies the proposers of the success of their Bid and invites them to sign a MOU indicating their willingness to accept the terms and conditions for Organising the AMPT conference.
