Venue Of Conference
VIT University Chennai campus is located in Tamilnadu, South India which is one of the most progressive states in India. It is strategically located South Chennai amidst IT, education and training institutions, and Techno parks. Chennai, formerly known as Madras, is the capital of Tamil Nadu, and is the fourth largest Indian city with a population of approximately 7.5 million.

Leading software companies such as IBM, TCS, Infosys, Wipro, CTS and Polaris have made a big presence in Chennai which is also known as the Detroit of South Asia since it is home to major automotive manufacturers such as Ashok Leyland, BMW, Daimler, Ford, Hyundai, Nissan, Renault, Caterpillar, etc. contributing to 30% of the country’s automotive industry and 60% of India’s auto exports.

Chennai airport is well connected to International destinations such as London, Frankfurt, Dubai, Singapore, Hongkong, Kualalumpur, etc. VISA procedure is easy and most tourists prefer Chennai for the local hospitality and cuisine (local as well as international).
